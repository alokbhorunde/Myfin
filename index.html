This page constitutes the Privacy Policy for the mobile application Myfin (referred to as "the Application", "our app", "we", "us", or "our").

When you use our app, you may share some information with us. We value your privacy and security, so we want you to know exactly what information we collect, how it is used, and the steps we take to protect it. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully so that you can make informed decisions about your use of our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

Personal Data

Personal Data refers to personally identifiable information that specifically identifies you as an individual (e.g., your name, email address, physical address).

We do not collect, store, transmit, or use any of your Personal Data through the Application.

If you voluntarily contact us by email for support or feedback, we will use your email address and any information you provide strictly for the purpose of responding to your communication. We will never share, sell, or use your email address for marketing purposes.

User Entered Data

Myfin is designed with privacy at its core. All financial and personal data entered into the Application by you, such as transactions, budgets, account details, and categories, is stored locally on your device.

We never transmit, access, collect, or use this data. Because the data remains exclusively on your device, we have no ability to read, share, or recover it if lost. We strongly recommend utilizing any available local or cloud backup features provided by your device's operating system (e.g., Google Drive or iCloud) to secure your data.

Device Data (Non-Personal Data)

Non-personal data refers to information that, by itself, does not personally identify you.

We may collect certain non-personal data automatically when you use the Application. This may include:

  • Device model number and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • Country code and language settings
  • General usage patterns and interactions within the app

We use this non-personal data exclusively for diagnosing technical issues, understanding general user behavior, and improving the overall user experience.

2. Third-Party Services

Myfin does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data from the Application is shared with or transmitted to any third party. The Application operates entirely offline with all data stored locally on your device.

3. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Since all user-entered data is stored locally on your device, its security is primarily dependent on the security measures you have enabled on your device (such as device PIN, biometric locks, and encrypted backups). We strongly encourage you to protect your device against unauthorized access.

By keeping your financial data entirely on your device and not on any remote servers, we eliminate the risk of your sensitive data being exposed in a server-side data breach.

4. Regional Compliance

India (DPDP Act, 2023)

Myfin aligns with the principles of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. We do not process any personal data, and all financial information remains under your sole control on your local device.

If you have questions about how these regulations apply to your use of Myfin, please contact us using the details provided below.

European Union (GDPR)

Since Myfin does not collect, process, or store any personal data on external servers, the Application is inherently compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). No personal data leaves your device, and there is no data controller or processor relationship between you and us beyond what is described in this policy.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our systems.
